Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product. For most spices and dried products, it's 1 ton, while for fresh produce, it's typically 5 tons. Contact us for specific product requirements.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on the destination and shipping method. Standard sea freight takes 15-30 days, while air freight takes 3-7 days. We provide real-time tracking for all shipments.

Do you provide quality certificates?

Yes, we provide comprehensive quality certificates including FSSAI, phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, and third-party laboratory test reports for all our products.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including Letter of Credit (LC), Telegraphic Transfer (TT), and Documents Against Payment (DAP). Payment terms can be negotiated based on the order size and relationship.

Can you provide custom packaging?

Absolutely! We offer custom packaging solutions including private labeling, custom bag sizes, and specialized packaging for different markets. Our team can work with your specifications.
