Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, you can call us directly or use WhatsApp for faster response.

Can I visit your facility?

Yes, we welcome visits from potential clients. Please schedule an appointment in advance so we can arrange a proper tour and meeting with our team.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for evaluation. Sample costs and shipping charges may apply, which can be adjusted against your first order.

What languages do you support?

Our team primarily communicates in English and Hindi. For other languages, we can arrange translation services as needed.
